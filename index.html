    Our Services

    Assesments.

    There are assesment centres in all the sub-counties in kenya where we assess pupils before enrolling to our school.

    Therapy.

    We also have a therapy room eqipmed with abductor seat,rollers, massage bed,walker and balances.We also engage a phsiotherapist from time to time to promote optimal mobility, physical activities and overall health and wellness of the pupils.

    Provision of Assistive Devices.

    Assistive device such as white cane and wheel chairs are available to pupils for mobility purposes.

    Curriculum.

    Our Curriculum covers a broad area including communication, social & literacy skills,sensory-motor intergration, envirinmental activities psychomotor activities,creative activities, numeracy activities, religous education and activities of daily living skills.

    Boarding Services.

    All our students are provided with Boarding services which give them a conducive learning environment.This include boy's dormitory, girl's domitory both with 24hrs matron and nurse, Dining hall with well Balanced diet food .

    Extra-carriculum.

    Our pupils practise sporting activities which enable them to keep fit.
