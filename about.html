About Us

A little about Us

Welcome to Kabarnet School for the Deaf and Blind Children.

The Kabarnet School for DeafBlind children was established in 1985.The School is a brainchild of the Late President of Kenya;H.E Daniel Toroitich Arap Moi.The School establisement wwas tranferred from Kuja in the former Nyanza Province to its current location in Kabarnet, Baringo County.

The Chriestoffel Blindenmission (CBM) did the initial constraction and sponsor the school untill the year Board of Management took over the Management of the school through the Ministry of Education. Board Of Management was put in Place to manage the institution.

Currently, the school enrolment stand at 75 pupils with 45 boys and 30 girls. The teaching staff members are 35 and non-teaching staff who over support service are 19.

The school admits students/learners from financially challenged backgrounds who are solely supported by grant from Ministry of Education Science and Technology. It is worthy to note that, the school cannot admit more learners despite the high demand due to resource constrains This is largely attributted to the fact that the initial support in form of grants from the Ministry of Education has Decline sharply from 2017, negatively affecting the smooth running of the school.

Mission

To Create a Conducive Environments that will ensure Equitable acess,Sustainability and Excellence in Learning and Career Development for deaf Blind pupils in the Region and beyond.

Vision

To Be centre of Excellence in provision of High quality education to every deaf blind learners in the school to adequately prepare them for careers and Life.

Our Mandate

To Promote, Disseminate and impact appropriate education techniques and tools to deaf and blind learners through provision of education and training sevices in collaboration with stakeholders for sociaeconomic empowerment.
